## Full description (max 4000 chars)
the Alculator works out which drink gives you the most alcohol for your money — no mental maths required.

Enter a drink's price, ABV% and volume, and it instantly ranks every drink you add by units of alcohol per £. The best value rises to the top, so you can see at a glance what's actually worth it.

WHAT IT DOES
• Ranks drinks by alcohol units per £ (higher = better value)
• Shows cost-per-unit and total units for every drink
• Animated value bars make comparisons obvious
• Multipack support — pick a can size, then ×4, ×6, ×8 or ×12
• Quick-fill presets for 330ml, 440ml, pints and 750ml bottles
• Switch volume units: ml, cl, litres or pints
• Tap any drink to edit it; swipe to delete
• Share your rankings as text with friends

DESIGNED TO BE SIMPLE
A clean, calm limewash design with no clutter. Everything happens on one screen.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
the Alculator collects nothing. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers and no internet connection — every calculation happens on your device.

HOW UNITS ARE WORKED OUT
Alcohol units = ABV% × volume (ml) ÷ 1000. Value = units ÷ price. It's the same standard UK unit measure used on drink labels.

Please drink responsibly. the Alculator is a value comparison tool for adults of legal drinking age. Know your limits — for guidance see the NHS alcohol units page.
