the Alculator

Privacy Policy · Last updated 26 June 2026

the Alculator ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. the Alculator does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts and asks for no permissions.

Where your information lives

The drinks you enter (price, ABV and volume) exist only in the app's memory on your own device while you use it. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Internet & third parties

The app makes no network connections. It contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party software development kits.

Children

the Alculator is a value-comparison tool intended for adults of legal drinking age and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
